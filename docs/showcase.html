Topology & time-travel ops

✅ Best for

  • Systems of record and audit trails

  • Private RAG / "ask your own documents," fully local

↔ Pair it for

  • High-contention multi-writer OLTP — writes are single-writer by design; pair with Postgres/SQLite (free)
  • Sub-millisecond transactional workloads
  • The PostgreSQL wire exposes read-only SQL; writes use CeQL
